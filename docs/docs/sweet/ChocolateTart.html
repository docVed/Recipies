Chocolate Tart

Filling adapted from Gourmet Traveller

Equipment

  • Small pot

  • Mug

  • Whisk

  • Sieve (ideally flat-bottomed for ease of use)

Ingredients

Ingredients in the order they are required.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 140 C.

  2. Add cream and milk to a small pot and heat on medium, then measure out chocolate separately into a heatproof bowl.

  3. When cream and milk start boiling, pour onto chocolate and leave for 30 s, then stir with whisk until uniform consistency.

  4. Add sugar and salt. Mix through.

  5. Crack eggs into a mug and beat them, temper eggs by adding two tablespoons of hot to it and mix through. Now stir in tempered egg mixture back into filling bowl and mixfilling

  6. Pour heated filing through a sieve into the blind-baked tart base.

  7. Cook for approximately 30 minutes in preheated oven. Check filling has been set by wobbling it, it should have a soft jelly consistency.
